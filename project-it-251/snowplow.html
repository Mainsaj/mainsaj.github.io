Snowplow Sam

The Snowplow Sam curriculum was developed by Learn to Skate USA for children ages 6 and under with little to no skating experience.

Snowplow 1

  1. Sit and stand up with skates on - off-ice
  2. Sit and stand up - on-ice
  3. March in place
  4. March forward (8-10 steps)
  5. March, then glide on two feet
  6. Dip in place

Snowplow 2

  1. March followed by a long glide
  2. Dip while moving
  3. Backwards walking (4-6 steps)
  4. Backward wiggles (6 in a row)
  5. Forward swizzles (2-3 in a row)
  6. Beginning snowplow stop motion
  7. Two foot hop in place

Snowplow 3

  1. Forward skating (8-10 strides)
  2. Forward one-foot glide (R and L)
  3. Forward swizzles (4-6 in a row)
  4. Backward swizzles (2-3 in a row)
  5. Moving forward snowplow stop (two-foot)
  6. Curves

Snowplow 4

  1. Forward skating
  2. Backward two-foot glide
  3. Backward swizzles (4-6 in a row)
  4. Rocking horse
  5. Two-foot turns (forward to backward, both directions)
  6. Two-foot hop
