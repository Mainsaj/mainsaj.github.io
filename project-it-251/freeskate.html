Free Skate

The Freeskate curriculum was developed by Learn to Skate USA for children ages 6 and up who have completed the Basic Skills curriculum.

Pre-Free Skate

  1. Forward inside open mohawk (R to L and L to R)
  2. Backward crossovers to backward outside edge (clockwise and counterclockwise)
  3. Backward outside edge to forward outside edge transition (clockwise and counterclockwise)
  4. Beginning step sequence
  5. One-foot upright spin (3 revolutions)
  6. Mazurka (R or L)
  7. Waltz Jump
    8. Bonus: Backward inside pivots (clockwise and counterclockwise)

Free Skate 1

  1. Forward power stroking (clockwise and counterclockwise)
  2. Forward outside and inside consecutive edges (4-6)
  3. Backward outside three-turns (R and L)
  4. Upright spin, back crossover entry (4-6 revolutions)
  5. Half flip
  6. Toe loop
    7. Bonus: Waltz jump - side toe hop - waltz jump sequence

Free Skate 2

  1. Alternating forward outside and inside spirals
  2. Backward outside and inside consecutive edges (4-6)
  3. Backward inside three-turns (R and L)
  4. Beginning back spin (1-2 revolutions)
  5. Half Lutz
  6. Salchow
    7. Bonus: Forward spiral variation

Free Skate 3

  1. Alternating backward crossovers to back outside edges (4 sets)
  2. Alternation mohawk/crossover sequence
  3. Waltz three-turns (clockwise and counterclockwise)
  4. Advanced back spin with crossed leg (3 revolutions)
  5. Loop jump
  6. Waltz jump-toe loop or Salchow-toe loop combination
    7. Bonus: Toe step sequence

Free Skate 4

  1. Forward power three-turns (R and L)
  2. Waltz eight
  3. Forward upright to backward upright spin combination (3 revolutions each foot)
  4. Sit spin (3 revolutions)
  5. Half loop
  6. Flip
    7. Bonus: Split jump, stag jump, split falling leaf

Free Skate 5

  1. Backward outside three-turn mohawk (clockwise and counterclockwise)
  2. Five step mohawk sequence (clockwise and counterclockwise)
  3. Camel spin (3 revolutions)
  4. Waltz jump - loop jump combination
  5. Lutz Jump
    6. Bonus: Loop-loop combination

Free Skate 6

  1. Forward power pulls (R and L)
  2. Creative step sequence
  3. Camel spin - sit spin combination (2 revolutions each position)
  4. Layback/attitude spin or cross-foot spin (3 revoltions)
  5. Waltz jump-half loop - salchow combination
  6. Beginning Axel
    7. Bonus: Backward outside pivot
