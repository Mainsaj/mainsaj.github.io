My name is Alexander Mains and I am a professional figure skating instructor. I coach all ages in the many skills of figure skating.

Figure Skating is challenging sport but with my many years of experience I can relay the knowledge required to not only accomplish these skills but also compete at a high level.

Not only is figure skating a form a physical activity but it also imbues qualities that are helpful in all aspects of life such as discipline and perseverance.
