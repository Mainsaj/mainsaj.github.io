Basic Skills

The Basic Skills curriculum was developed by Learn to Skate USA for children ages 6 and up with little to no skating experience.

Basic 1

  1. Sit and stand up - on-ice
  2. March forward
  3. Forward two-foot glide
  4. Dip
  5. Forward swizzles (6-8 in a row)
  6. Backward wiggles (6-8 in a row)
  7. Beginning snowplow stop
    8. Bonus: Two-foot hop in place

Basic 2

  1. Scooter pushes (R and L)
  2. Forward one-foot glides (R and L)
  3. Backward two-foot glide
  4. Rocking horse
  5. Backward swizzles (6-8 in a row)
  6. Two-foot turns forward to backward (Clockwise and Counterclockwise)
  7. Moving snowplow stop
    8. Bonus: Curves

Basic 3

  1. Beginning forward stroking (correct use of blade)
  2. Forward half swizzle pumps on a circle (6-8 in a row, R and L)
  3. Moving forward to backward two-foot turns (clockwise and counterclockwise)
  4. Beginning backward one-foot glides
  5. Backward snowplow stop (R and L)
  6. Forward Slalom
    7. Bonus: Forward pivots (clockwise and counterclockwise)

Basic 4

  1. Beginning forward stroking (correct use of blade)
  2. Forward half swizzle pumps on a circle (6-8 in a row, R and L)
  3. Moving forward to backward two-foot turns (clockwise and counterclockwise)
  4. Beginning backward one-foot glides
  5. Backward snowplow stop (R and L)
  6. Forward Slalom
    7. Bonus: Forward pivots (clockwise and counterclockwise)

Basic 5

  1. Backward outside edge on a circle (R and L)
  2. Backward inside edge on a circle (R and L)
  3. Backward crossovers (clockwise and counterclockwise)
  4. Advanced two-foot spin (4-6 revolutions)
  5. Hockey stop
    6. Bonus: Side toe hop (R and L)

Basic 6

  1. Forward inside three-turn (R and L)
  2. Moving backward to forward two-foot turn on a circle (clockwise and counterclockwise)
  3. Backward stroking
  4. Beginning one-foot spin (2-4 revolutions)
  5. T-stops (R and L)
  6. Bunny hop
  7. Forward spiral on a straight line (R an L)
    8. Bonus: Shoot the duck (R or L)
